## SCENE 4: Vendor Dashboard & Catalog Management (1:15 - 1:45)

### Visual
- Show vendor dashboard with premium header
- Display catalog page with product grid
- Show quick actions menu floating button
- Demonstrate adding a new product

### Narration
"Your vendor dashboard is your command center. Upload your inventory with detailed specifications—carat weight, clarity, color, certification for diamonds; gemstone type, treatment, origin for gems; and metal type, weight, design details for jewelry. Upload multiple high-quality images and manage everything in one place."

### Workflow Steps

#### Adding Products
1. Click floating Quick Actions Menu
2. Select "Add Product" 
3. Choose product type (Loose Diamonds / Gemstones / Jewellery)
4. Fill in detailed specifications:
   - **Diamonds**: Carat, Clarity, Color, Cut, Polish, Symmetry, Fluorescence, Lab Certification
   - **Gemstones**: Type, Shape, Weight, Treatment, Origin, Enhancement
   - **Jewellery**: Name, Metal Type, Purity, Net Weight, Diamond/Gemstone Details
5. Upload product images (up to 3 images based on plan)
6. Set pricing (Cost Price, Retail Price, Gold Rate for jewellery)
7. Add stock quantity and SKU
8. Save product

#### Managing Catalog
- View all products in grid layout with filtering
- Filter by category (Jewellery, Gemstones, Diamonds)
- Search products by name or SKU
- Edit existing products
- Soft delete products (moved to trash, recoverable)
- Pagination with "Load More" for large catalogs

## SCENE 5: Creating & Sharing Catalog Links (1:45 - 2:05)

### Visual
- Click "Share Catalog" from quick actions

- Display share analytics dashboard

### Narration
"Share your catalog with clients using smart shareable links. Set custom markup or markdown pricing for different customer segments, choose which product categories to include, set expiry dates for seasonal promotions, and track every interaction with detailed analytics."

### Workflow Steps

#### Creating Share Link
1. Click "Share Catalog" from Quick Actions Menu
2. Configure share link settings:
   - Select product categories to share (All / Jewellery / Gemstones / Diamonds)
   - Set markup or markdown percentage (custom pricing for this link)
   - Choose expiry date
   - Toggle "Show Vendor Details" (display business info)
3. Generate link
4. Copy shareable URL
5. Download QR code for offline sharing (JPG/PNG)
6. Share via WhatsApp, email, or social media

#### Share Link Features
- Unique URLs for different clients
- Custom pricing per link (markup/markdown)
- Category-specific sharing
- Time-limited access with expiry
- Vendor branding customization
- QR codes for physical distribution

## TECHNICAL NOTES FOR VIDEO PRODUCTION

### Visual Style
- **Color Scheme**: Gold (#E5B85B), Purple (#C087F0), Blue (#87D4F0)
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (serif) for headings, Inter for body
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll reveals, 3D tilt effects, floating particles
- **Theme**: Dark mode with elegant jewelry aesthetic

### Transition Effects
- Smooth fade transitions between scenes
- Scroll-based animations
- Parallax background effects
- 3D card tilt on hover
- Animated icon reveals

### Key UI Elements to Highlight
- Floating Quick Actions Menu
- Animated statistics counters
- 3D product viewer
- Theme switcher (light/dark mode)
- Interactive cards with tilt effects
- Scroll-triggered animations
- Parallax sections
- Animated particles in hero

### Screen Recording Tips
1. Use high resolution (1920x1080 minimum)
2. Show smooth mouse movements
3. Pause on important features (2-3 seconds)
4. Use cursor highlights for clarity
5. Demonstrate hover effects
6. Show mobile responsive design
7. Capture loading animations
8. Record form interactions completely

### Audio Recommendations
- Professional voiceover with clear enunciation
- Background music: Elegant, professional, not too loud
- Sound effects: Subtle UI sounds for clicks/actions
- Volume balance: Voice 70%, Music 30%

### Pacing
- Total duration: 3 minutes
- Introduction: 30 seconds
- Feature overview: 1 minute
- Detailed workflows: 1 minute 30 seconds
- Closing: 10 seconds
- Keep narration concise and engaging
- Match visuals precisely with narration timing
